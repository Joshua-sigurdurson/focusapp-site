Getting Started

  1. Open Focus App and grant Screen Time authorization when prompted.
  2. Select distracting apps/categories you want to block during focus sessions.
  3. Start a Pomodoro or Infinite Focus session.

Troubleshooting

  • If blocking doesn’t apply, restart the session and ensure authorization is granted.
  • Make sure your device is updated to the latest iOS version supported by the app.
