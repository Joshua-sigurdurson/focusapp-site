Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Summary: Focus App is a self-control and focus tool. We do not read content from other apps, and we do not collect screenshots or screen recordings.

Screen Time APIs

Focus App uses Apple Screen Time APIs (FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity) only to enforce focus sessions requested by the user. Users explicitly select distracting apps or categories to block during focus sessions.

Data We Collect

  • Minimal focus session metadata generated by our app: session start time, end time, duration, and interruption/allowance events.
  • We do not access or upload system Screen Time usage details for other apps.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. If the user opts into cloud sync or social features (e.g., leaderboards/duels), we may upload only aggregated focus session results (e.g., total duration), not system Screen Time usage details.
